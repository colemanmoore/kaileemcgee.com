ABOUT KAILEE MCGEE

Kailee McGee is one of our more talented, creative female entrepreneurs. She is a freelancer writer, author, artist, and international filmmaker.

Ms. McGee is a New York University Tisch School of the Arts alumni, who received high honors and acclaim even at young age for her written words and motion pictures.

She lives in Los Angeles, California with her cat, Supper, and drives a blue 2014 VW Golf.
