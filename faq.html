I was very popular. A huge part of the drama program. Editor-in-Chief of the newspaper, The Brush and Palette, my junior and senior year. Some of the best years of my life. Most of the stars from the hit MTV show, Laguna Beach: The Real Orange County, continue to be some of my closest friends.
